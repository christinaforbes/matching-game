Periodic Table of Elements Matching Game

How to Play

  1. Each card has a front and a back. The front contains an element's symbol and name, while the back is a solid color. All cards are displayed face down by default.
  2. Click/tap a card to select it. The card will be flipped to reveal the front. Click/tap another card to check for a match.
  3. If the cards are a match, then they'll be hidden from view. If the cards aren't a match, then they'll be flipped face down again.
  4. Continue selecting cards until no cards remain onscreen. When all the cards have been hidden, you've won the game.
